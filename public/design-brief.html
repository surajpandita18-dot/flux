Anti-Patterns (never do these)

Never: Clinical gray background

No #F3F4F6 (gray-100) or similar neutral gray as app background. Always warm cream #FBF8F5.

Never: Phase color on CTAs

Pastel pink, pastel sage, pastel blue on a button. CTAs are always near-black (#1A1814) or rose (#E8627C) only.

Never: Pastel text on pastel bg

text-menstrual (#F4A0B4) on bg-menstrual-soft (#FDF2F5) = 1.8:1 contrast. Always use #5C5754 for labels on tinted cards.

Never: Sub-11px text

9px or 10px text is WCAG failure. Minimum 11px for labels, 12px for body, 14px for readable content.

Never: Girlboss pink or fuchsia

Not hot pink, not fuchsia, not Barbie. The rose is #E8627C — muted, warm, not a saturated statement.

Never: Text nav links as bottom bar

Small underlined text links at screen bottom are not navigation. Always render the fixed 5-tab BottomNav.

Product Context (for AI prompting)

Product purpose

Flux makes the menstrual cycle legible and useful in everyday life. It tells you what phase you're in, what that means for your energy, food, and mood today, and gives you one actionable insight. It does not diagnose, does not add anxiety, and does not track for tracking's sake. It replaces the vague awareness of "I'm having a week" with "I'm in the luteal phase — this is normal, here's what helps."

User

Women 22–35, urban, smartphone-first. Using Flux during quiet personal moments: morning coffee, post-workout wind-down, end of day. They want to feel understood by their body, not managed by an app. Non-technical; they respond to warmth and clarity, not clinical dashboards or data.

Brand tone

Warm, clear, grounded. Not clinical. Not girlboss-pink. Not generic wellness. The app should feel like a knowledgeable friend who happens to understand women's health deeply and speaks to you like an adult.

Anti-references

  • Clue: too clinical, too data-heavy, feels like a medical spreadsheet
  • Flo: too pink, too gamified, too fertility-obsessed for non-TTC users
  • Generic wellness: gradient orbs, vague "namaste" copy, empty animations
  • SaaS dashboards: charts everywhere, metric obsession, no emotional warmth

Claude Design Prompt

Copy this prompt into Claude Design

Design the home screen for Flux — a menstrual cycle tracking app for women 22–35.

PRODUCT: Flux tells users what phase they're in (menstrual, follicular, ovulation, luteal) and gives one actionable insight per day. No diagnosis, no anxiety, no data dashboards. It replaces "I'm having a week" with "I'm in my luteal phase — this is normal, here's what helps."

TONE: Warm, clear, grounded. Like a knowledgeable friend, not a medical app. NOT clinical Clue, NOT pink Flo, NOT gamified wellness.

SCREEN (home, menstrual phase active, Day 3 of 28):

1. GREETING — "Hi, Anjali" (28px, 900 weight, #1A1814) + "How do you feel today?" (14px, #A8A4A0)

2. PHASE RING CARD — SVG donut ring on white card (#FFFFFF, 24px radius, soft shadow):
   - 4 arc segments (menstrual pink #F4A0B4 FULL opacity, follicular sage #8CCBA8 35%, ovulation gold #F5D07A 35%, luteal periwinkle #A8C4E8 35%)
   - Mascot centered inside: abstract cat with sleepy eyes, body color #FECDD3, ear #FDA4AF, accent #F43F5E
   - Day number "3" (36px, 900 weight) above mascot
   - Below ring: "Rest Mode" (13px, 700, #5C5754) and "Day 3 of 28" (11px, #A8A4A0)

3. CTAs:
   - Primary: "Check in now" — full width, 54px height, 16px radius, background #1A1814, white text, 700 weight
   - Secondary: "🩸 My period started" — full width, 50px height, 16px radius, background #E8627C, white text

4. CALENDAR — white card, 24px radius:
   - Month header "April 2026" (13px bold) + small legend (Period · Ovulation · Next)
   - Monday-first 7-column grid, 32px day cells
   - Period days (1–5): #F4A0B4 background, white text, rounded-full
   - Ovulation days (13–16): #F5D07A background, #1A1814 text, rounded-full
   - Today (day 3): #E8627C background, white text, rounded-full
   - Predicted next period (27–30): rgba(244,160,180,0.2) bg, #F4A0B4 text
   - Footer: "Next period in 25 days · 28 May"

5. PHASE CARD — soft rose background #FDF2F5, 24px radius, no border:
   - Dot (#F4A0B4) + label "REST MODE" (11px, 700, uppercase, #5C5754)
   - One-liner: "Your body's doing a lot right now — rest isn't laziness, it's literally what this phase is for." (15px, #1A1814)

6. QUICK STATS — 3-column grid, each cell #FDF2F5 background, 16px radius:
   - 🪫 Low / Energy | 🧘 Gentle / Movement | 🌙 Low / Fertility
   - Emoji (22px) → value (13px bold #1A1814) → label (11px #5C5754)

7. BOTTOM NAV — fixed, 80px, warm cream #FBF8F5/95 + blur, 1px border #F0EBE6:
   - 5 tabs: Home · History · Log · Partner · Settings
   - Active tab (Home): #E8627C
   - Inactive: #A8A4A0
   - Center "Log" tab has circular + icon slightly larger

DESIGN RULES:
- App background: #FBF8F5 (warm cream, NEVER gray or pure white as background)
- No pure #000 or #fff — always tinted
- No phase colors on CTA buttons — only #1A1814 or #E8627C
- No saturated fills — pastels only for phase backgrounds
- No gradient text, no glassmorphism, no hero-metric grids
- Font: system UI stack (-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, system-ui)
- Touch targets minimum 44px
- Minimum text size 11px

Show light mode only. Mobile viewport 375×812px.

- Build effort: 5 = easy (1 sprint), 1 = very hard (3+ sprints)
- Privacy risk: 5 = no risk, 1 = high risk (pregnancy data is sensitive)
- Revenue potential: 5 = clear paid feature, 1 = hard to monetize

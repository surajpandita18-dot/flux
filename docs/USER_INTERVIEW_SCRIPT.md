# Flux — User Interview Script
# Sprint 4 | 20 minutes | SAGE

## Before you start
- Record with permission (voice note on phone is fine)
- Don't explain features — let them tell you what they understood
- If they pause, wait. Silence = thinking = gold.
- Your job: listen, not pitch.

---

## Opening (2 min)

"Thanks for doing this — I'm building Flux and your honest take means more than anything.
There are no wrong answers. I want to know what's actually working and what isn't.
Can I record this? Just for my notes — won't be shared."

---

## Section 1 — First impression (3 min)

1. "When you first opened Flux, what did you think it was going to do?"
   *(Listen for: expectation vs reality gap)*

2. "What was the first thing you actually did in the app?"
   *(Listen for: where they went first — onboarding friction, confusing flow)*

3. "Was there anything that felt confusing or took you a second to figure out?"
   *(Listen for: UX blockers — don't defend, just note)*

---

## Section 2 — Daily use (5 min)

4. "How many times have you opened Flux in the last week? What made you open it?"
   *(Listen for: pull vs push, what triggers them)*

5. "When you see the phase card, what do you actually do with that information?"
   *(Listen for: does it change behavior? Is it just info or is it actionable?)*

6. "Have you used the daily log — energy, mood, symptoms? If yes, why? If no, why not?"
   *(Listen for: friction, habit formation, perceived value)*

---

## Section 3 — The core thesis (4 min)

7. "Has Flux changed anything about how you think about your week or your body? Even small things."
   *(This is the money question — pause after asking it. Let them think.)*

8. "Is there a moment where Flux felt genuinely useful — like it gave you something you didn't expect?"
   *(Listen for: the 'aha' — if it exists, this is what you double down on)*

---

## Section 4 — Partner feature + missing pieces (4 min)

9. "Did you try the partner feature? What was your reaction to it existing?"
   *(Listen for: comfort level, privacy instinct, relationship dynamics)*

10. "What's the one thing Flux doesn't do that you wish it did?"
    *(Listen for: V2 signals — pregnancy, calendar, patterns, notifications)*

---

## Closing (2 min)

"Last thing — if you were telling a friend about Flux in one sentence, what would you say?"
*(Listen for: how they describe the value — this is your word-of-mouth copy)*

"Is there anything you wanted to say that I didn't ask about?"

"This has been incredibly helpful. Can I follow up if I have a quick question?"

## Patterns to watch across 5 interviews

If 3+ say the same thing → it's real, act on it.
If 1 person says something surprising → note it, don't act yet.
If nobody mentions partner feature → low priority for V2.
If everyone mentions the same missing feature → bump it to top of V2.
